Our story

Simba Supermarket is built for modern grocery shoppers.

We combine a premium online shopping experience with fast delivery, trusted products, and a simple checkout path from sign up to payment.

Our mission

To deliver fresh, high-quality groceries and household essentials with the easiest online shopping experience. Every item is selected to make kitchen and home life better for every Rwandan family.

What we offer

  • Complete category browsing for groceries, beauty, and home care
  • Secure sign up, sign in, cart, and checkout workflows
  • Multiple payment options: MOMO, Airtel Money, Cards, Cash on Delivery
  • 24/7 AI chatbot support for instant help

Quality promise

Every product is presented with clear pricing, category detail, and a polished layout so customers feel confident from their first view to order completion. Fresh produce daily, trusted brands, and excellent service.
